Ways to Maintain a Postive Mental Health

  • Getting professional help if you need it. (You can go visit the Resources page to get help)
  • Take prescribed medications
  • Connecting with others you feel good with. It can be your family, friends, or a community
  • Getting physically active (this can include taking a walk or playing a sport with friends)
  • Helping others through volunteering or community service
  • Getting enough sleep (9 hours is recommended for teens, 8 hours for adults)
  • Mindfulness and breathing (breathing excersise is below)
  • Do a hobby or something you find fun!
  • Draw or color a coloring book
  • Avoid caffine
  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Drink enough water (8 cups is recomended)
  • limit social media usage

Breathing Exercise:

This simple breathing exersise can help calm yourself in stressful and anxious situations: inhale slowly through the nose and exhale slowly through the mouth and repeat for several minutes until relaxed.
